DIY tools refer to a range of tools that are designed for use in do-it-yourself projects, repairs, and home improvement tasks. Here are some common categories of DIY tools:

Hand tools: Hand tools are designed to be used manually, and include items such as hammers, screwdrivers, wrenches, pliers, and chisels.

Power tools: Power tools are electrically or battery-powered tools that are designed to make tasks easier and more efficient. Examples include power drills, saws, sanders, and nail guns.

Measuring and layout tools: Measuring and layout tools are designed to help DIYers accurately measure and mark materials. Examples include tape measures, levels, squares, and rulers.

Safety equipment: Safety equipment is designed to protect DIYers from injury while working on projects. Examples include safety glasses, ear protection, gloves, and dust masks.

Painting and decorating tools: Painting and decorating tools are designed to help DIYers achieve a professional finish on their projects. Examples include paint brushes, rollers, scrapers, and sandpaper.

Garden tools: Garden tools are designed for use in outdoor DIY projects, such as landscaping and gardening. Examples include shovels, rakes, pruning shears, and hedge trimmers.

Choosing the right DIY tools for a project is important to ensure that the work is completed safely and effectively. DIYers should also take care to properly maintain their tools, such as cleaning and sharpening them, to ensure their longevity and effectiveness.
