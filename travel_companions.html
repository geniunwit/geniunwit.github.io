Travel Companions

You are in good company

Travel Accessories • June 28, 2023 • Tim Konv

Here are some travel accessories that you might find useful:

Travel Pillow: A good travel pillow can make a long flight or car ride much more comfortable. Look for one that is soft and supportive, and can be easily compressed for storage.

Packing Cubes: Packing cubes are a great way to organize your luggage and make it easier to find what you need. They come in different sizes and colors, and can help you save space and avoid wrinkles.

Travel Adapter: A travel adapter is a must-have for international travel, as it allows you to use your electronics in foreign countries. Look for one that is compact and works in multiple countries.

Portable Charger: A portable charger can be a lifesaver when you’re traveling and need to keep your devices charged. Look for one with a high capacity and multiple USB ports.

Luggage Scale: A luggage scale can help you avoid overweight baggage fees by allowing you to weigh your luggage before you get to the airport. Look for one that is compact and easy to use.

Noise-Canceling Headphones: Noise-canceling headphones can help you block out the sounds of crying babies or noisy passengers on a plane or train. Look for ones that are comfortable and have a long battery life.

Travel Wallet: A travel wallet can help you keep all your important documents and cards organized and easily accessible. Look for one with multiple pockets and RFID-blocking technology to protect against identity theft.

Foldable Water Bottle: A foldable water bottle is a great way to stay hydrated on the go without taking up too much space in your luggage. Look for one that is durable and leak-proof.

Travel Umbrella: A compact travel umbrella can come in handy when you encounter unexpected rain or inclement weather. Look for one that is lightweight and sturdy.

TSA-Approved Lock: A TSA-approved lock can help keep your luggage secure while allowing airport security to inspect it if needed. Look for one with a combination lock and a sturdy cable or shackle.
